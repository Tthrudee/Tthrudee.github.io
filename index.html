What is GA:TC?

GA:TC or Gene Altered Tactical Combats is a 3D Action RPG game.

Story

Game Story: It is the year 2045, a global organization called the Gene Federation aims to improve humanity's evolution through the use of new technologies to modify DNA at a faster and easier rate. However, testing this new method would be seen as unethical to the public, and the federation has been secretly testing this method on humans for the past couple of years. This has lead to unexplained and dangerous side effects within the subjects who were tested. The federation has locked down these genetic experiments to hide them from public view until their method has been perfected, but they won't be kept secret for much longer. What is the federation doing to the DNA of these people that is making them so dangerous?
